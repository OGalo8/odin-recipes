Homemade Pizza

This homemade pizza recipe features a soft, chewy crust topped with a flavorful tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. It's simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion. Serve with a side of marinara sauce for dipping or a fresh salad.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Spices and herbs
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with herbs and spices.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
